FAQs

How do I cancel my order?

RESERVATIONS

How Do I Change My Reservation?
All you have to do is go to the “My Account” tab and tap “Reservations,” find the reservation you'd like to change and make your updates. We'll follow up with an email confirming your changes to both you and the restaurant.
How Do I Cancel My Reservation?
Tap your way over to the My Account tab and tap Reservations. Find the one you'd like to cancel and, well, cancel it. We'll send you an email confirming your cancelation.
You've Answered All My Questions. What Do I Do Now?
Well, it's clearly the restaurant's loss. We'll help you find a host of other restaurants ready to cater to your every whim so you'll never be hurt like that again. *hugs*

LOYALITY POINTS

Are Loyalty Points Anything Like Brownie Points?
Yes! Almost exactly. You earn Loyalty Points with pretty much every action you take on Munch Ado and you can totally use them to get (actual) brownies, other free food, and exclusive rewards and specials.
How Do I Earn Loyalty Points?
Lots of ways! So many ways that you probably already have some you didn’t even know about. Go on check. We’ll wait. You can track them on the My Account tab under the My Points section – creative name. For an actual answer to all the ways you can earn points, please consult the Loyalty Points page.
What Are These Point Levels?
There are four levels – each with their own perks. Everyone starts out on level one, as you’d expect. As you earn more points and do more stuff, you reach new levels with bigger and better perks. Read all about them.

DINE & MORE, SPECIALS, REWARDS, AND COUPONS

What is Dine & More?
Dine & More is a set of rewards programs exclusive to individual restaurants where you can earn extra points for the actions you take with them and redeem all of your points for exclusive specials and rewards.
How Do I Join a Dine & More Rewards Program?
When you order or visit a restaurant with a Dine & More rewards program, they'll give you a Dine & More card with a unique code you can use to join.
What Is A Dine & More Special?
These are offers that are exclusive to you as a member of a restaurant’s Dine & More rewards program. They come in many forms from secret menu items to discounts on orders or reservations.
How Do I Use My Munch Ado Coupon?
When you receive a coupon on Munch Ado, you’ll see it at the top of the homepage. You can use this coupon towards deliciously discounted food at participating restaurants. Keep an eye out for the [icon] icon to see which restaurants accept the coupon.

THE MUNCH ADO FEED

What Is the Munch Ado Feed?
The check ins, reviews, photos you and your friends post live in the Munch Ado Feed. It’s where food adventures and dining experiences are shared and commented on.
How about a refund after I've canceled my order?
That’s totally up to you. You know what else is up to you? What they see. Update all your share settings in the Feed tab by tapping the lock icon.
Who Are These People in My Feed?
They’re your friends! The “Others” feed is all the updates from all your Munch Ado friends including what they’re trying, where they’re eating and what they’re craving.

PAYMENTS

So, Tip Wise, What's the Etiquette There?
Well, tips are encouraged but not mandatory. At some restaurants, we even help calculate the tip as you place items into your cart but we won't force you to include it during checkout. For others, you can leave a tip on the restaurant’s physical receipt. We do hope you'll tip generously.
Why No Love for COD (Cash on Delivery)?
We love COD, our finance guys though... they're sticklers. Sorry everyone.

NOTFICATIONS

How Do I Change My Notification Preferences?
On the My Account tab, tap Update Profile and then go to Notification Settings and choose your favorite way to keep up to date on your Munch Ado life.
Munch Ado Emails Are Going to My Junk Folder. How Can I Fix This?
Well, that’s no good. All you should have to do is mark our emails as A-Okay and "Not Junk" to make sure we show up in your inbox. You could also add WeCare@MunchAdo.com and Notifications@MunchAdo.com to your address book.
